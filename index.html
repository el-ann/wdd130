Ghana

Ghana, located in West Africa, is known for its rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes. It boasts vibrant cities like Accra, the capital, and historical sites such as Cape Coast Castle. Renowned for its warm hospitality, Ghana is also famous for its cocoa production. The country celebrates a mix of traditions and modern influences, reflected in its festivals, music, and cuisine, making it a unique destination for both visitors and locals alike.
